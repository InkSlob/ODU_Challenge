Goals & Requirements

The goal of this competition is to automate analysis and report generation of video for managers of construction sites. Currently GB of data are generated daily and stored, but a human has to watch the videos for analysis. The reasons workers watch these videos (outlined below) need to be incorporated into a video analytics platform. The Project must demonstrate the real world and useful data within a construction environment.
